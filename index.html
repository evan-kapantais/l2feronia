Gaming with Feronia

Welcome to L2 Feronia. We are a small group of friends who used to play Lineage 2 during its golden era. During the quarantine, nostalgia hit us hard and we came up with the idea of starting our own private L2 server.

For this past month we have been trying our best to develop and maintain a stable server free of bugs, unbalanced classes and stable economy, but we can't achieve that without a solid player base. As a result, we are inviting you to join L2 Feronia's open beta stage.

Please note that after the beta test is over all characters will be deleted, but the players' accounts that were created during this phase will automatically be turned into premium accounts for one month.
